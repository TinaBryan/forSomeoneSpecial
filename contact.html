Availability

If you are in great need such that your event is coming quickly and your precious flower girl does not have her tutu, give me a call. I will do my best to accommodate if possible. Due to the custom nature of my business, I do not usually have 'ready made' items in stock. Depending upon what you need verses the orders I have at the time of your order it may still be possible. *

Once your order has been confirmed please allow 4 - 6 weeks for delivery. Standard shipping rates apply - U.S.P.S. Priority mail $12 (US Only)

*If expedited shipping is necessary due to a late notice order the invoice will reflect an additional shipping charge at the purchasers expense.

High volume seasons may require extra time for delivery so please order as soon as possible allowing sufficient time for your items to be crafted and delivered.

  1. Prom & Wedding seasons begin ~ February
  2. Fall Wedding & Christmas seasons begin ~ September
