Tutu Room

It all begins within the heart, and I know nothing more that will stir the heart of every little girl like the Waltz of the Snowflakes from the Nutcracker performed by the Royal Ballet.

Who could ever resist dancing while wearing a tutu?

I truly could not say.

As a child I grew to deeply love the ballet. My sister and I danced in the Nutcracker year after year. We will always be ballerinas at heart.

Truth be told, we'd still love to dance but.. I'm not sure any ballet company would have us. (The smell of mentholated pain rub may tip them off.. but you never know!)

What I know is... deep within the heart of every girl is knowing truly they are a royal princess.

Even the most athletic girls jump a little higher while wearing a fluffy tutu.

One thing to always keep in mind: a princess comes in all shapes and sizes including the furry kind! Each princess is a 'One of a kind' made for a special purpose.

So, live it out! Dance to your hearts content because you are someone special!
